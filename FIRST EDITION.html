WHAT IS CISTM

The College of Information Systems and Technology Management (CISTM) consolidates IT education at PLM, offering BS Computer Science, BS Information Technology, and Master in IT. Formed in 2024 under the Academic Cluster, CISTM focuses on hands-on learning, research, and industry readiness. Students participate in coding bootcamps, cybersecurity challenges, networking projects, and tech seminars. The college emphasizes innovation, teamwork, and problem-solving to prepare graduates for real-world IT careers.

WHAT IS PLM SITE

The Society of Information Technology Enthusiasts (SITE) at PLM is a student organization promoting IT skills and projects. SITE conducts workshops, hackathons, app development competitions, and cybersecurity training. Members gain mentorship, exposure to emerging technologies, and networking opportunities. SITE fosters collaboration, creativity, and professional development for IT students at all levels.

WHAT IS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Information Technology (IT) involves using computers, software, and networks to collect, process, store, and transmit information. IT powers business systems, communications, healthcare solutions, and entertainment. Fields include software development, database management, cybersecurity, AI, cloud computing, and networking. IT is crucial for automation, problem-solving, and innovation, shaping the modern digital world.

CAREER PATHS & OPPORTUNITIES

BSIT graduates can work as Software Developers, Network Administrators, Database Analysts, IT Consultants, Cybersecurity Specialists, or pursue entrepreneurship. They may join startups, tech companies, government agencies, or research institutions. Continuous learning, certifications, and specialization in AI, cloud computing, or cybersecurity enhance career prospects and open global opportunities in IT fields.

FUTURE INNOVATIONS

IT students are engaged with emerging technologies like AI, blockchain, cloud computing, IoT, and mobile development. They create solutions for automation, data analysis, cybersecurity, and digital innovation. These innovations shape smart cities, healthcare systems, finance, and entertainment. Students are prepared to lead projects and contribute to technological advancement worldwide.
